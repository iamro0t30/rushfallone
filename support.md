# Rushfall One Support

Need help with Rushfall One?

Contact support:

iamroot94@gmail.com

## Purchase Issues

If a Silver purchase succeeded but Silver was not added to your account, please include:

- Your account email, if you used login.
- The Silver pack you bought.
- The approximate purchase time.
- The Apple purchase receipt or transaction information, if available.

## Account And Cloud Save

If progress or Silver does not appear after reinstalling the app, make sure you are logged in with the same account used for the purchase or cloud save.

## Gameplay Issues

For gameplay bugs, please include:

- Device model.
- iOS version.
- What happened.
- What you expected to happen.
- Screenshots or screen recordings, if useful.
